Under the Hood

Built on Serious
Technology

Shariah.AI is not a wrapper. It's a purpose-built Islamic finance intelligence platform powered by RAG, LLMs, and a curated scholarly knowledge graph.

01

Large Language Models (LLMs)

We leverage state-of-the-art LLMs fine-tuned on Islamic finance corpora — including AAOIFI standards, scholarly books, and published fatawa — to generate contextually accurate, source-grounded responses.

02

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

Our vector database indexes thousands of Shariah texts, enabling the AI to retrieve relevant passages before generating answers — grounding responses in real Islamic scholarship, not hallucination.

03

Shariah Knowledge Graph

A structured ontology of Islamic finance concepts — linking rulings, scholars, madhabs, AAOIFI standards, and financial instruments — that powers our compliance engine beyond simple text matching.

04

Real-Time Financial Data

Integration with live market data feeds enables real-time halal stock screening, nisab calculation based on current gold/silver prices, and up-to-date sukuk analysis.

05

Multi-Madhab Reasoning

Our system doesn't collapse complex scholarly disagreements into a single answer. It surfaces Hanafi, Maliki, Shafi'i, and Hanbali positions where they differ — respecting the diversity of Islamic jurisprudence.

06

Scholar Review Pipeline

High-stakes outputs go through a human scholar review loop before delivery — ensuring critical compliance decisions are always validated by qualified Islamic finance experts.
